IServe Inc.,

Redefining Performance

IServe Inc., made its humble beginnings about ten years ago. Then it had only two products, Hydrokon, a unique water de-scaling device and RunMax, a fuel-saving and engine performance booster. But both these products were unique and created a positive buzz in the industry. Now, ten years down the line, IServe Inc., has grown to become one of the leading suppliers and manufacturers of material handling equipment.

When we started, our clientele consisted mainly of central government bodies but today we supply equipment to the industrial sector, local authorities and all major supermarket chains. Having worked on many projects we have earned the reputation for efficient manufacturing and accurate installation on time and within budget and working drawings are always issued for your approval.

Our core strength is technology and our technicians have been selected, not only on their expertise in the design and manufacturing but also in their enthusiasm for finding solutions to seemingly insurmountable problems. Our design department uses the advanced software package for 3D modeling of our equipment. This has led to considerable shortening of our delivery times and enhanced our output quality.

No design leaves IServe Inc., until it has undergone extensive testing. Our testing procedures make sure that our equipment satisfies the client’s functional requirements. We also provide expert on-site commissioning services throughout the world. Complementing our engineering expertise, the use of most advanced manufacturing techniques helps us produce high-quality equipment. Using high precision equipment IServe Inc., is geared to cater to the varying requirements of a demanding market.

Guided by relentless focus, we believe in the policy of FIX IT AND FORGET IT, ie our products are custom designed with low maintenance, longevity and high performance in mind. We strongly feel that this is the best way to keep the customer happy. We will constantly strive to implement the critical initiatives required to achieve our vision. In doing this, we will deliver operational excellence in every corner of the Company and meet or exceed our commitments to the many constituencies we serve. All of our long-term strategies and short-term actions will be moulded by a set of core values that are shared by each and every associate. And that is what we call, redefining performance.
